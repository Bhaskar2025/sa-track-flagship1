#D1 - The Architect Mindset

## What does an architect do differently froma senior engineer?
Senior engineer thinks within the service boundary. Architect start deciding the system
before team is setup. Architects decides consistency model, trade offs for availability, latency, 
maintainability, agility, feasibility etc. How different services would interact and how databases
are structured and what we have to give up.

## Architecture vs Design
Design is something which can be decided and changed within the team without affecting other.
Architecture is something which affects multiple systems, how they interact and they live
for years in the system and hard to change. 
Architecture defines service boundaries and constraints and design lives within those constraints.

## What does a product company SA owns that a consulting SA doesn't?
A product company SA owns the production consequences of their architecture decisions while
the consulting SA moves to next project when the architecture is finalized.
